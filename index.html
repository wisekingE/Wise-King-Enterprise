How to Make Your Golden Latte

Add one tablespoon to warm milk, stir and sweeten. That is all it takes to start your day the right way.

01

Warm 250ml of your preferred milk — dairy, oat, almond, soy or coconut

02

Add 1 heaped tablespoon (approx. 8g) of Wise King Golden Latte Blend

03

Whisk, stir or froth until fully dissolved and beautifully golden

04

Sweeten to taste with honey, maple syrup or your favourite sweetener

05

Also delicious iced — blend with cold milk and ice for a refreshing golden drink
